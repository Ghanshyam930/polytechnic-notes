About us

Founded in 2025, PolyNotes began as a small initiative to help polytechnic students access quality study materials. Our founders, noticed the challenges students faced in finding organized, reliable resources for their diploma courses.

What started as a personal collection of notes has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of students across multiple branches. We're proud to have created a community where knowledge is shared freely and learning is made accessible to all.

Today, PolyNotes offers complete syllabus coverage, previous year papers for all semesters of polytechnic education.
